Use the mouse wheel to scroll up or down. If the start or end of the time line has been reached, this will automatically zoom in. You can also zoom in and out explicitly with the scroll wheel by holding the shift key or pressing the '+' or '-' key.

Clicking an area will zoom in or out to the corresponding period; clicking a link will open the corresponding article in this frame. Clicking the time scale on the left side will zoom out to the full time line. Clicking the image will show a larger version in this frame including the image attribution.
